Our History

Founded in 2024 by Aisha Khamis Masaka, a trailblazing footballer from Tanzania who became the first female from the country to play in the British Female Premier League. Inspired by her journey and the obstacles she overcame, the foundation aims to provide access to quality education and use sports as a tool for empowerment.

Our Mission

To provide quality education and sustainable solutions by fostering personal and community development through sports and games. Our core values in this mission include Environmental Responsibility, Hygiene Education and Healthy Habits and Breaking The Silence : Empowering Girs through improved menstrual Health Management

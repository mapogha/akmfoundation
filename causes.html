Our Key Initiatives

Breaking barriers and creating opportunities for girls in Tanzania

Girls' Education Equity

Breaking educational barriers for girls in Tanzania through improved learning conditions and school materials.

Safe & Dignified Schools

Building 60 toilet holes across 10 schools to improve attendance and menstrual management.

Menstrual Health Management

Providing sanitary pads and hygiene education to reduce school absenteeism among girls.

Sports for Empowerment

Using football to foster confidence, teamwork and leadership among Tanzanian youth.

Gender Norm Shifting

Promoting equality through advocacy and showcasing empowered female role models.

Ending Gender-Based Violence

Improving school infrastructure and awareness to protect girls' safety and rights.

Featured Campaign

Amka Malkia Kipaji na Elimu

"If you educate one girl, you have educated the nation." Our flagship program addresses multiple barriers to girls' education through comprehensive support including school materials, sanitation facilities, and mentorship.

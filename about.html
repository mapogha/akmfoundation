About Us

The Aisha Khamis Masaka Foundation (AKM Foundation) is a Tanzanian non-governmental, non-profit organization dedicated to empowering marginalized communities through quality education and the life-changing influence of sports and games. Founded in 2024 by celebrated Tanzanian football star Aisha Khamis Masaka, the Foundation was born from a deeply personal story of resilience, determination, and hope.

Our Programs & Initiatives

Creating impact through comprehensive community programs

Education Programs

  • Free school kits (uniforms, books, stationery)
  • After-school tutoring for underprivileged students
  • Digital literacy and computer training

Sports for Development

  • Community football tournaments for girls and boys
  • Life-skills training through team sports
  • Fitness and health awareness programs

Women and Youth Empowerment

  • Entrepreneurship training for young women
  • Mentorship programs led by successful female athletes
  • Micro-grant programs for community business initiatives

Health Outreach

  • Period poverty campaigns and pad distribution
  • Basic health checks and screenings
  • Hygiene and wellness education in schools
